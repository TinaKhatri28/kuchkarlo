The Core Problem

The Employability Crisis

Universities excel at delivering theory, but they fundamentally fail to create job-ready engineers. In India alone, over 80% of STEM graduates are deemed unemployable by the tech industry.

Students are Lost

They lack structured guidance, struggling to navigate what skills actually matter in a rapidly shifting market, leading to severe interview anxiety and high rejection rates.

Colleges are Blind

Placement cells run on antiquated spreadsheets. They have zero quantifiable data on which students are actually prepared for technical interviews before companies arrive.

Companies Bleed

Recruiters spend millions manually filtering thousands of inflated resumes, only to find candidates lacking basic practical skills during expensive technical screens.

The Market Gap

Current Solutions are Fragmented

Job Boards (LinkedIn, Naukri):

Purely transactional. They help you apply, but do absolutely nothing to help you build the skills necessary to actually pass the technical interview.

B2C Product Deep-Dive 1

Building Habit through Gamification

We solve the notorious EdTech drop-off problem (where 90% abandon video courses) by applying deep psychological engagement loops to technical skill-building.

XP & Streaks

Students earn XP for completing daily coding modules. Streak mechanics (inspired by Duolingo) ensure they log in consistently, building long-term coding habits rather than cramming.

Leaderboards

Healthy competition among peers, branches, and rival colleges drives intensive upskilling far better than traditional grades. Students fight for the top spot in the 'State Coding League'.

Corporate Badges

Verifiable corporate skill badges are unlocked as students pass threshold assessments, signaling elite, validated competence directly to our hiring partners.

B2C Product Deep-Dive 2

24/7 Autonomous AI Mentors

Every student gets a personalized, highly contextual AI tutor. This allows us to scale infinite, personalized guidance without massive operational overhead costs.

Live Code Review Agent

The AI doesn't just give the answer. It acts like a senior engineer, analyzing code in real-time, guiding students through syntax errors, optimizing algorithmic time complexity (Big O), and explaining complex concepts at 3 AM via natural language.

Hyper-Realistic Mock Interviews

Voice-and-video enabled AI avatars conduct full technical and HR rounds. The AI dynamically adapts follow-up questions based on student responses, delivering a comprehensive diagnostic report on communication confidence, code accuracy, and optimization.

B2B Product Deep-Dive 1

The JET Engine

The Job Eligibility Test (JET) fundamentally replaces the antiquated resume. It is our proprietary, heavily proctored assessment framework that corporate partners explicitly trust.

Anti-Cheat Protocols

Browser lock-down, eye-tracking via webcam, tab-switching alerts, and randomized algorithmic question generation guarantee that scores accurately reflect the student's unassisted capability.

The Readiness Score

An aggregated 0-100 metric reflecting a student's exact probability of clearing an interview. This allows universities to identify weak spots months before a corporate hiring drive begins.

Pre-Vetted Trust

Companies using our platform allow students who score 85%+ on JET to completely bypass initial resume screening and standard technical phone screens, accelerating hiring.

B2B Product Deep-Dive 2

The Corporate Hiring Portal

We don't just train students; we give companies direct ATS-integrated access to a pre-vetted, highly skilled talent pool. Companies can post jobs and hire with zero friction.

Direct Job Postings & ATS Integration:

Companies post full-time roles and internships exclusively to our university partners. Our platform integrates seamlessly with Workday, Greenhouse, and Lever for frictionless candidate export.

Skill-Based Sourcing (Bypassing Resumes):

Recruiters bypass the noisy resume pile. They filter candidates instantly based on verified platform XP, completed GitHub-linked projects, and AI mock interview scores.

One-Click Recruitment Drives:

Launch massive campus hiring drives virtually. The platform automatically handles the assessment dispatch (via JET), automated shortlisting, and interview scheduling workflows.

B2B Product Deep-Dive 3

The TPO Command Center

For universities, we replace messy spreadsheets with an intelligent SaaS command center that gives placement officers (TPOs) absolute oversight over their campus readiness.

Readiness Analytics

Instantly see what percentage of the Computer Science batch is ready to clear a Microsoft or Amazon interview based on aggregated AI mock scores and JET results. Identify struggling students before companies arrive.

Zero Friction Integration

Fully cloud-hosted SaaS architecture with immediate '.edu' Google Auth SSO. Completely white-labeled to match the University's domain and branding, providing a premium portal for alumni networks.
